# Import Calendar

Once the user is logged in, the application can import the user's calendar from Google Calendar.
The application can only process events that have been imported into a user's google calendar via the [Visual Schedule Builder Export](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/visual-schedule-builder-e/nbapggbchldhdjckbhdhkhlodokjdoha?pli=1) Chrome extension.

## Steps to Import Your Calendar

1. Install the [extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/visual-schedule-builder-e/nbapggbchldhdjckbhdhkhlodokjdoha?pli=1)

> The extension can only be downloaded on Google Chrome
1. Sign into your desired Google account on Google Chrome.

> It is recommended to either use an account with an empty calendar or a new dummy account to not interfere with your personal calendar
1. Connect to your [Student Center](https://campus.concordia.ca/psp/pscsprd/EMPLOYEE/SA/c/SA_LEARNER_SERVICES.SSS_STUDENT_CENTER.GBL?gh_deep_link=1)
2. Navigate to the Visual Schedule Builder
3. Select the Winter 2026 semester
4. Export the Visual Schedule Builder calendar into your desired Google Calendar via the export button
5. Open the app and log into your google account with which you exported your schedule to
6. Import your Calendar via the Import Calendar option in the hamburger menu

> As of February 21st (PR [#94](https://github.com/C0dingViking/soen390-mini-capstone/pull/94)), importing your calendar will only print information to the console.
